Lyon Invitational JR Girls 2M
 Batesville, AR -- October 2, 2008
Due to a significant number of runners running the wrong route, the JR Girls' event was officially declared a "no-time" race.

If there are any coaches who, to a degree of certainty, know that his/her JR Girl runner ran the entire course, please send the names and finish times to gtb@arkansasrunner.com and they will be posted.  One week will be allowed for collection of times before posting.

Runner name and time will be compared to the select times that were captured at the finish line before publication.
